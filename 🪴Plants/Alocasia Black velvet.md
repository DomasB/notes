## Care 
- 🌞 Bright indirect light
- 💧 Medium
-  🧪 5.5-6.5 pH
- 🪴 Well-draining coarse mix 
-  🌱 Little fertilizing
-  🌡️ 12-26C
-  🌫️ 60% - 70%
-  ☠️ Toxic
-  ➗ Rhizome division
